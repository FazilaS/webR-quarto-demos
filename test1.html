GLobal Remittance Outlook 2022

In the digital age, remittances serve as a vital artery in the global economy, pumping billions of dollars annually across borders. They represent not just monetary transactions but also the hopes, dreams, and sacrifices of millions of migrants striving for a better life and supporting their loved ones back home. These financial lifelines, these remittances, weave a story of sacrifice, resilience, and love that transcends borders and connects hearts across the globe. Figure 1 highlights this

Remittance refers to the act of sending money, typically by a person who has migrated from their home country to another, back to their family or loved ones in their country of origin. It’s essentially a transfer of funds from one place to another, often to provide financial support or assistance to those who remain in the migrant’s home country.
